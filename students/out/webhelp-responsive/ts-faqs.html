Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions by AirClassroom users.

Is there a limit on how many reservations I can book within a week?

Yes, there is a weekly limit on reservations. AirClassroom users can book no more than 3 reservations per week, and no two reservations can be on consecutive days. This helps prevent only a few users having access to the rooms throughout the week.

How many people are allowed in an AirClassroom at once?

Shanks 360 and 180 are very similar in size. We generally don't have any problems with users overpacking rooms with people, but suggest that they limit the size of their party to 25. There are only 20 computers per room, so that gives everyone a computer and chair with 5 people who either have to double up or use the space creatively.

What do I do if I want to move furniture, desks or other items around the room to customize my AirClassroom to best fit my needs?

AirClassroom spaces are intended to serve as multi-purpose, versatile facilities for our users. If you want to move something around to make the space better fit your needs, go for it! The only thing we ask is that you rearrange the room to its original configuration before leaving.
