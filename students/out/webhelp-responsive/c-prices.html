Prices and Booking

To book with AirClassroom, there are certain fees and requirements.

Prices

When you book a reservation with AirClassroom, the price of your reservation is determined by how many hours you plan on using the classroom at a rate of $25/hr.
Example:
If you want to use Shanks 360 for 4 hours, the price for your reservation will be $25 x 4, or $100.

Requirements

  • - When you book with AirClassroom, you must provide a valid method of payment.
  • - You must book a reservation for 1-8 hours, between the hours of 9 a.m. and 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.
  • - You can only book one room at a time when using AirClassroom.
