Using the Computers

The computers found in Shanks 360 & 180 are powerful resources you will want to take advantage of.

Here are instructions on how to use AirClassroom computers.
  1. Turn on the computer by pressing the "Power" button.
    You will be automatically redirected to the home screen without needing to enter a username or password.
  2. Select the program you would like to use from the Applications folder or desktop toolbar.
    Your program will start and you will be ready to go!
  3. IMPORTANT STEP: When saving, do so with a personal flash or hard drive.
    Program data on AirClassroom computers is erased at the end of each day, so your files will not be saved unless you do so on a separate personal drive.
  4. When you're finished working, turn of the computer by clicking on the "Apple" icon and selecting "Shut Down".
You will have have the basic knowledge of how to start up and shut down the AirClassroom computers, as well as vital information about saving your important files.
