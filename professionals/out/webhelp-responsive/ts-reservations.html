Cancelling or Rescheduling Reservations

Have a problem with your reservation and need to cancel? Something come up and you need to reschedule? Here's what to do.

Cancelling a reservation with AirClassroom

Here at AirClassroom, we understand if you need to cancel your reservation because of a problem, conflict or other issue. We just ask that you notify us at least 24 hours before the time/date of your reservation.

When cancelling your reservation, contact us at airclassroom@vt.edu or 1-800-AIRVT at least 24 hours before the time/date of your reservation. We will email or call you back confirming your cancellation within 24 hours. Your reservation will be refunded in full if you abide by these conditions.

Rescheduling a reservation with AirClassroom

If you need to reschedule your reservation, legitimate reasoning must be provided. Your options may also be slim, depending on how close to your original reservation time you request a reschedule.

You must notify us at least 3 days before the day of your reservation to reschedule, and we will provide you with a list of potential time/date slots available. Again, the earlier the better when it comes to rescheduling. Legitimate reasoning MUST be provided.
