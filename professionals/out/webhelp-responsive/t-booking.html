Booking a Reservation

Information on how to book a reservation with AirClassroom.

If you have an account, follow these instructions to book a classroom for use. If you don't have an account, please see our page titled "Creating an Account"
  1. Log in to your AirClassroom account
  2. Select "New Reservation" from the Main Menu
  3. Select a date and time range you would like to reserve a classroom
  4. Provide us with a brief description of what you plan on using the classroom for
  5. Enter a valid method of payment or select a saved method (returning customers)
Boom! Your reservation is booked with AirClassroom. We'll send you a confirmation email as well as provide you with a printable confirmation page for your records.
All you have to do now is show up to the classroom you rented on the day and time you reserved it for, and leave when your reservation expires! Simple as that.
